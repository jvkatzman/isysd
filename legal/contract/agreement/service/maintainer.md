# Maintainer

A maintainer of a project, especially a guld app, is responsible for triaging change requests. This requires project management, quality assurance, and content matter expertise.

### Responsibilities

 + Respond to unanswered change requests within <%= contract.responseTime %> hours.
 + Run unit tests, linting and any other standard tests against the change request.
 + Manually review any non-templated changes.

### Pay

Each maintainer will be paid <%= contract.amount %> monthly for each month they are approved by at least <%= contract.approval %> % of the pre-existing XP. The XGC is to be issued out of the community Equity account.

### Experience

All XGC pay earned in this way will be matched one to one with XP. XP issued will come out of the community Experience account.
